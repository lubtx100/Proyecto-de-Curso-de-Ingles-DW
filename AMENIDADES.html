The amenities of a beach resort

Find out why we are one of the best beachfront resorts in Costa Rica

Perfectly located on the beach of Tamarindo, Tamarindo DIRIA Beach Resort has a vibrant environment. From the moment you arrive at our beachfront hotel, our staff is dedicated to ensuring an unforgettable experience.
Three distinctive areas of our resort offer the experience you are looking for. Our family-friendly lagoon pool, nestled among a stunning tropical garden and pre-Columbian sculptures, offers plenty of fun.
Tamarindo beach offers some of the best sunset views in Costa Rica, you can enjoy it in our oceanfront pool, located next to the children's pool. If it's quiet and tranquility you're looking for, our Tropicana area offers a peaceful pool scene with loungers, sun loungers, and a swim-up bar.
Designed to delight and inspire at every turn, Tamarindo DIRIA Beach Resort offers a Costa Rica vacation experience like no other.
